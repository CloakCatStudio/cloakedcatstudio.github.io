Games Under Development

Project: Grab n' Slash is the project title of the 2D platformer being developed. The game is centered around the idea of choice through mechanics. What that means is rather than binary text options, the game allows players to make narrative choices with their actions. The story will be determined by what the player chooses to do or not to do.

The game has no set schedule for launch. Updates and more information can be found on the Development Log page.
